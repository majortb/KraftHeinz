Finance QC recommendations implemented

The calculator now distinguishes proven savings from upside, uses dynamic AI consumption, includes failed automation penalties, and exports an assumption audit trail.

Hard-dollar discipline

Automation savings, application retirements, and recontact reduction are separated from productivity and commercial upside. Charts label source-of-value quality instead of mixing everything into one undifferentiated return.

Outcome-based logic

The model calculates AI-resolved contacts and failed automation cost. Deflection is not counted as savings unless it becomes a resolved consumer outcome with a recontact control.

Audit-ready assumptions

Every slider has an include checkbox, source, and confidence rating. Scenario changes preserve the model structure and export the assumptions, formulas, and calculation outputs.
